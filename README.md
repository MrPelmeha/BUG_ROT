# BUG_ROT

LORE: 

A monopoly company that sells seeds to farmers (and controls the food sector of economy) is trying to create a food shortage crisis through the introduction of an invasive species of bug. Their facility is located underground. 

The goal is to create a controllable force that can eat away at competitors (small and large), that, if needed, can be stopped whenever. 

Playing with nature never works, as the project slowly loses control over it's experiments. 

The scientists working on the project want to stop the longer it goes on, but their bosses refuse to listen.

Your goal is to eat, evolve and, eventually, escape.

GAME LOOP:

You start of in a glass enclosure. It's night and the lab has been closed for the day. You mature faster than was expected (your first mutation), you end up laying an egg(?) and escaping through a dislodged vent. 

You explore the midnight laboratory, collecting genetic material through fighting other bugs, and or eating. 

EVERYTHING you consume will give you a benefit and drawback called a MUTATION. You must balance these two factors to achieve the best build. You can get stronger (main) MUTATIONS through fighting other bugs (mini-bosses) or (extra) MUTATIONS through consuming food (plants, dead bugs etc.) and finding special rooms. 

(e.g. You gain a percentage of speed, and lose a percentage of health). Perhaps you may also gain mutations that allow you access to certain rooms and blocks passage to others. 

The end of a level is marked by you exiting into the hall leading to the exit. The lab finally detects a breach and you end up fighting the security (humans/machines). If you win they end up containing you, setting you back to the first level (the enclosure). 

Next loop you play as the next generation of the bug. Some of the MUTATIONS may change or disappear. 

Every loop you're placed into a more secure enclosure, but there is always a way for you to escape. And with every loop the lab becomes more hostile and more disorganized and out of control. 

The END of the game can appear on the 3rd (?) round. At this point you are a danger to the scientists. After defeating them, you escape through a vent, escaping into the lush fields.
